CIK¶

The CIK class allows users to get company filings using company tickers and/or company names. For example, the code below fetches the past 15 10Q reports for the tickers AAPL, MSFT, and FB (Apple, Microsoft, and Facebook).

In [1]: from secedgar.filings import Filing, FilingType, CIK

In [2]: my_ciks = CIK(['aapl', 'msft', 'fb'])

In [3]: my_filings = Filing(cik=my_ciks, filing_type=FilingType.FILING_10Q, count=15)

The CIK class can also look for CIK values by using the company name.

In [4]: from secedgar.filings import Filing, FilingType, CIK

In [5]: my_ciks = CIK(['Apple Inc.', 'Microsoft Corp', 'Facebook'])

In [6]: my_filings = Filing(cik=my_ciks, filing_type=FilingType.FILING_10Q, count=15)

A mix of tickers and company names is also allowed.

In [7]: from secedgar.filings import Filing, FilingType, CIK

In [8]: my_ciks = CIK(['aapl', 'msft', 'Facebook'])

In [9]: my_filings = Filing(cik=my_ciks, filing_type=FilingType.FILING_10Q, count=15)
